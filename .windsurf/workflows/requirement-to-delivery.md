# Requirement to Delivery Workflow

This workflow guides you through the complete process from initial requirement gathering to final delivery of a change or feature.

## Steps

1. **Requirement Gathering** — Collect and capture requirements from stakeholders
   - Schedule stakeholder interviews
   - Document initial requirements
   - Identify key user personas and use cases
   - Capture business objectives and success criteria

2. **Requirement Clarification** — Refine ambiguous or incomplete requirements into clear, actionable statements
   - Review gathered requirements for clarity
   - Ask clarifying questions for ambiguous items
   - Convert vague requirements into specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, time-bound (SMART) statements
   - Identify and document assumptions and constraints

3. **ADR Generation** — Produce Architecture Decision Records documenting key technical choices
   - Identify architectural decisions needed
   - Evaluate alternative approaches
   - Document decision context, options, and outcomes
   - Create ADRs for significant technical choices
